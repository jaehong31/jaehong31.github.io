I'm a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the MURGe-Lab (UNC-NLP Group) at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, working with Prof. Mohit Bansal. Prior to joining UNC, I received my Ph.D. degree from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in 2023, advised by Prof. Sung Ju Hwang, and won the best Ph.D. Dissertation Award from the KAIST College of Engineering and School of Computing. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST).

My research broadly focuses on building robust AI systems that can reliably and safely understand and interact in a dynamic, ever-changing multimodal world.
