Professor


Jaehong Yoon is an assistant professor in the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS) at Nanyang Technological University.

Prior to joining NTU Sinpapore, he was a postdoctoral research associate at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, working with Prof. Mohit Bansal and completed his Ph.D. in the School of Computing at KAIST in 2023, advised by Prof. Sung Ju Hwang.

- To prospective Ph.D. students


If you are interested in pursuing a Ph.D. under my supervision, I typically expect applicants to have at least one first-authored paper at a top-tier conference in ML/AI/NLP, such as ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, ICCV, ECCV, CVPR, EMNLP, ACL, or NAACL.

Applicants should also demonstrate a strong background in machine learning, deep learning, or related areas, and I am planning to recruit students who are interested in timely and impactful research in ML/NLP/multimodal AI, including World Models, Embodied AI, Open-ended Learning (Openendedness), Post-Training Techniques, Coding Agents, and Trustworthy AI, but not limited to these areas.
