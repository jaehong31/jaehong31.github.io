Openings


I am looking to hire multiple PhD students to join the COSMO (COgnitive and Adaptive Systems for MultimOdal Intelligence) Lab at NTU Singapore, the college of computing and data science (CCDS), starting in Spring or Fall 2026. Kindly note that due to the volume of applications, I may not be able to respond to everyone individually. Thank you for your understanding.

To prospective Ph.D. students


If you are interested in pursuing a Ph.D. under my supervision, I typically expect applicants to have at least one first-authored paper at a top-tier conference in ML/AI/NLP, such as ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, ICCV, ECCV, CVPR, EMNLP, ACL, or NAACL.

Applicants should also demonstrate a strong background in machine learning, deep learning, or related areas, and I am planning to recruit students who are interested in timely and impactful research in NLP/ML/multimodal AI, including World Models, Embodied AI, Open-ended Learning (Openendedness), Post-Training Techniques, and Trustworthy AI, but not limited to these areas. To apply to join the group, please complete this Google form.

For details on the official Ph.D. application process, please refer to the information provided Here.

To prospective interns


We also welcome applications from highly motivated undergraduate or master's students looking for research internship experience in a related field. The ideal candidate will have strong programming skills (e.g. Python, PyTorch), a solid background and research experience in ML/AI/CV/NLP.

To apply to join the group, please complete this Google form.
