Jaehong Yoon

Assistant Professor, NTU Singapore

Jaehong Yoon is an Assistant Professor in the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS) at Nanyang Technological University.

Prior to joining NTU, he was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, working with Prof. Mohit Bansal. He received his Ph.D. from KAIST in 2023, advised by Prof. Sung Ju Hwang.

Prospective PhD students

Fall 2026 intake

If you are interested in pursuing a PhD under my supervision, I typically expect applicants to have at least one first-authored paper at a top-tier conference in ML, AI, NLP, such as ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, ICCV, ECCV, CVPR, EMNLP, ACL, or NAACL.

Applicants should also demonstrate a strong background in machine learning and deep learning. I am particularly interested in candidates who are excited about timely and impactful research in CV, NLP, and multimodal AI, including world models, embodied AI, open-ended learning, post-training techniques, coding agents, and trustworthy AI (not limited to these areas).
